My Commonplace

This must be the place to organize information: those items that are static in nature. Movies, books, quotes that will remain the same throughout the test of time but that I might come back to again and again with my persona changed and developed by the years. That is what separates this from a diary. This is not a record of the different people I have been, but a record of the people that strike a chord along my journey.
